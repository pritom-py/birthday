# Missing Assets for Full Experience

To hear the sounds and music, please add the following audio files to your `public/` folder:

1.  `happy-birthday-short.mp3` - Background music.
2.  `clap.mp3` - Clapping sound effect.
3.  `pop.mp3` - Balloon/Heart pop sound.
4.  `win.mp3` - Game win fanfare.
5.  `bonk.mp3` - Punch/Bonk sound effect.
6.  `funny-cry.mp3` - Funny cartoon crying sound.
7.  `kick.mp3` - Kick sound effect.
8.  `woosh.mp3` - Flying away sound effect.

**Note:** The app will work visually without them, but you will see errors in the console and hear no sound.
